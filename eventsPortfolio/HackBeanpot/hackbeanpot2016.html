HackBeanpot 2016

HackBeanpot is a nonprofit that runs hackathons in the Boston area. The hackathons are focused on introducing new hackers into the hackathon community through small, local, inclusive hackathons. Events generally consist of ~200 attendees with multiple sponsors.
