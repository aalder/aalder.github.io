Over the past 5 years, I have worked full-time, part-time and volunteered on a multitude of different events, and have worked many in many different roles and capacities. My strengths in planning is my focus on optimizing the attendee experience. For every event, each attendee should have a unique and meaningful experience they remember for a lifetime. The little details are the key to achieving that wow factor, and I bring that detail focused vision to all the events I work on. Due to my computer science degree, I often excel at producing events in technical or related fields, such as esports tournaments and anime conventions.
